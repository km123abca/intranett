CentralEIS is a web enabled Employee Information System, developed for Central Govt.departments spread all over India. The no. of DDOS under GOI is 12,000 (approx) and no. of working Central ovt. employees is around 48 lacs. Currently DDOs are using different type of applications for generating paybills. These are, ComDDO, MicroSoft Excel or any other customised application developed for paybill. The tools have been developed by SDU,NIC Pune, to port the data in non-EIS format to EIS format. This document will enable the DDOs to prepare the data in EIS format.
