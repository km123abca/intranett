Smt Anu Jose IAAS, Deputy Accountant General (Social and General Sector II)

The functional wing Social and General Sector II in the O/o AG (GSSA) is involved in the audit of
  • Higher Education Department
  • General Education Department
  • Cultural Affairs Department
  • Housing Department
  • Labour and Skills Department
  • Law Department
  • Kerala Water Authority
  • Administration of Justice including High Court of Kerala
  • Autonomous Bodies
  • Public Sector Undertakings
  • Certification of Externally Aided Projects and State/Centrally Sponsored schemes


Smt. Anu Jose IAAS, Deputy Accountant General is in charge of Social and General Sector II in the Office of the AG (GSSA). Her various responsibilities include;
  • Audit of Government Departments in General and Social Sector
  • Preparation of Audit Reports relating to General & Social Sector
  • Separate Audit Reports for autonomous bodies and Supplementary Audit Reports for PSUs
